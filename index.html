Our History

Welcome to Methodology, a place where warmth meets wit, and every sip brings an unforgettable flavor adventure. We are a home for true coffee lovers, who understand that coffee is not just a drink, but also an experience. We started our journey with one goal in mind to create an environment that embraces the diversity of coffee cultures from all corners of the world. With sincere passion and deep love for quality coffee beans, we opened our doors at 05 June 2021. Our story begins with a passionate coffee fan, Jonathan Situmorang, who through his life's journey discovered the magic hidden in every cup of coffee. With his strong vision to provide a special coffee experience, Jonathan and his team built Methodology as a destination for coffee lovers who want to explore and appreciate the art of coffee making. At Methodology, we not only serve high-quality coffee but also provide a comfortable and welcoming space where customers can relax, connect, and find peace in simple moments. We present a unique combination of tradition and innovation, by selecting the best coffee beans from around the world and processing them with advanced techniques to produce extraordinary taste. Enjoy your coffee, and welcome to Methodology.
